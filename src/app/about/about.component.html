ABOUT FEENIX


Feenix was launched in June 2017 as a response to the
  #FeesMust Fall movement that spread across campuses
  in South Africa during 2015 and 2016. This movement
    highlighted the extremely high cost ol tertiary
      educationand the impact financial stress has on
                          student success rates

The Feenix team is young . diverse and passionate about
  education. We believe that access to education should
  not be dependent on wealth. Our founding partner ,
    Standard Bank shares in this belief and without their
      financial support and guidance , Feenix would not be in
                             existence today

  Feenix is a Public Benefit Organisation (930057053)
      and governed by a Trust (IT831/2017).
